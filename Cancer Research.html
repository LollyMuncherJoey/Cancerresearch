You've got a friend in me

Kids Helping Kids fight cancer

Cancer is scary it's scary for those whose fighting cancer and for family and friends. Today in the UK alone around 11 children and young people are diagnosed with cancer. Having friends is really important especially when life gets tough. It is extra important to have someone you love around you when are sick and sad.

We want to help you, help your friends

There are two ways our website can help

1. Our website can provide tips,resources and links to help you be a good friend.

2. Donate to play our games all the money goes to cancer research.
